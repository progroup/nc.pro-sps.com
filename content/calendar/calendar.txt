From this page you can get updates on North Carolina Training and TA initiatives, learn about trainings and other events, and even register or access registration.

## Add your event

You can add your  events here, i.e. Public Training, Health Fair, etc.
Please click the link, complete the form, and allow at least 4 business days for posting. Keep in mind this is only for block grant public events. Workgroup meetings and other internal operations should not be posted.
